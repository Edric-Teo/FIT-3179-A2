What is HTLV 2.0 Rating?

Rating is a relatively simple concept based
on statistics that HLTV introduced back in CS:GO.
It aims to improve upon the skewed
K/D Ratio used by Valve to rank
player's performance throughout a match.
It ranges from 0 to 2.0 with
1.0 being the average.
