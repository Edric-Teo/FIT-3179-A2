What is HTLV 2.0 Rating?

HLTV 2.0 Rating is a relatively simple concept
based on statistics that HLTV introduced back
in CS:GO. It aims to improve upon the
skewed K/D Ratio used by Valve to rank
player's performance throughout a match.
It ranges from 0 to 2.0 with
1.0 being the average.

For example, K/D can be skewed towards
a player that prefers to play safe. So,
they may die less often and get a higher
K/D Ratio as they would have more
kills than deaths in that match.
It would be better to include additional
information to prove that the player is
performing better than his peers or opponents.

Most Common Weapons Used by Pro Players

More than 60% of the chart contains either Rifles or Sniper Rifles , 25% starting pistols and less than 15% SMGs. The main rifles: AK-47 and M4A1-S are widely used as they are reliable, cost-effective and versatile in most situations. The starting pistols being close are due to them being free at the start of each round. Whereas the MP9 is the best gun for eco-rounds where CTs usually want to gamble on a cheap SMG and exchange it for a better rifle if they manage to kill a Terrorist.

For the AK-47 has the most kills in professional play due to its ability to one-shot headshot any opponent from close to long range.

For the CT-side, although they have a choice between the M4A4 or M4A1-S, the latter is often favored as it has a silencer, more damage and is cheaper by 200$ in game. So, it would be used way more than its non-silencer counterpart.
